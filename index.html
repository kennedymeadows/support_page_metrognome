Support & Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the tempo?
Use the tempo slider to adjust the BPM (beats per minute). You can drag the slider or tap the + and - buttons to change the tempo in smaller increments.
Can I use the app while my phone is locked?
Yes! The metronome will continue to play even when your phone is locked. Just unlock your phone to adjust settings or stop the metronome.
What's the tempo range?
The app supports tempos from 30 BPM to 300 BPM, covering everything from slower practice sessions to fast-paced exercises.
Can I change the sound?
Yes! Tap the settings icon to choose from different metronome sounds and customize the volume to your preference.
Does the app work without an internet connection?
Absolutely! The metronome works completely offline. No internet connection required.
Can I set a time limit?
Yes, you can set practice sessions with a timer. Choose your duration and the metronome will automatically stop after the set time.

Troubleshooting

❌ No sound from the metronome
✓ Check that your device volume is turned up. Ensure the device isn't in silent mode (check the side switch on iPhone). Try restarting the app or your device.
❌ Metronome stops unexpectedly
✓ Make sure your device's battery saver mode isn't killing the app. Check app permissions in your device settings. Try closing other apps running in the background.
❌ Sound quality issues or crackling
✓ Reduce the volume slightly to prevent distortion. Close other apps that might be using audio. Restart the app.
❌ App crashes when I open it
✓ Force close the app completely and reopen it. Clear the app cache in your device settings. If the problem persists, try reinstalling the app.
❌ Tempo feels inconsistent
✓ This can happen if your device is running low on resources. Close background apps and restart your device. Make sure battery saver mode is disabled.

Tips & Tricks

  • Slow practice: Start with a slow tempo to build muscle memory, then gradually increase speed.
  • Accents: Some sounds include an accent on the first beat—great for practicing rhythm patterns.
  • Headphones: Using headphones gives you a clearer click and doesn't disturb others.
  • Practice timer: Set timed sessions to track your practice consistency.
